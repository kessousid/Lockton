# Lockton Insurance Platform — Demo Walkthrough Script

Use this script to record a video demo. On Mac, open **QuickTime Player → File → New Screen Recording**.

Record each persona separately or as one continuous video (~8-10 minutes total).

---

## PART 1: Admin Persona (~3 min)
**Login:** `admin@lockton.com` / `admin123`

### 1.1 Dashboard (30s)
- Show the 5 KPI cards (Total Clients, Active Policies, Pending Claims, Revenue, Retention Rate)
- Scroll to charts: Policy Distribution pie chart, Monthly Premium Trends line chart
- Show Claims by Status bar chart, Renewal Pipeline chart
- Point out the alerts section at the bottom

### 1.2 Client Management (30s)
- Click **Clients** in sidebar
- Use the search bar to filter clients
- Change the status filter dropdown
- Click on **"Apex Manufacturing Co."** to see client detail
- Show KPI cards, contact info, cross-sell opportunities
- Show the policies and claims tables

### 1.3 Settings & User Management (30s)
- Click **Settings** in sidebar
- Show the user list with roles
- Click **"Add User"** button — show the form (don't need to submit)
- Click **"Roles & Permissions"** tab — show the 4 role cards
- Click **"Audit Log"** tab — show recent activity

### 1.4 Analytics (45s)
- Click **Analytics** in sidebar
- Show **Overview** tab with 4 charts
- Click **"Loss Ratio"** tab — show industry comparison
- Click **"Broker Productivity"** tab — show broker metrics
- Click **"Revenue Forecast"** tab — show 12-month projection

### 1.5 Workflows (30s)
- Click **Workflows** in sidebar
- Click on **"Renewal Reminder Workflow"** — show the steps visualization
- Show the tasks list with status badges
- Click on **"Claims Processing"** workflow to show different steps

### 1.6 Notifications (15s)
- Click the **bell icon** in the header
- Show the notification dropdown with renewal alerts, pending claims, at-risk clients

**Logout** (click Logout in sidebar)

---

## PART 2: Broker Persona (~3 min)
**Login:** `broker@lockton.com` / `broker123`

### 2.1 Dashboard (15s)
- Briefly show the dashboard (same data, broker perspective)

### 2.2 Policy Management (45s)
- Click **Policies** in sidebar
- Show the policy list with filters (type, status)
- Use search to find a policy
- Click on a policy to see detail page
- Show premium, deductible, coverage limit cards
- Show policy details and client information panels
- Click **Edit** — show the edit form

### 2.3 Claims Management (45s)
- Click **Claims** in sidebar
- Show the analytics KPI cards (Total Claims, Approval Rate, Avg Cycle Time)
- Show the Claims by Status chart and Status Pipeline
- Click on a claim row to open the detail modal
- Show the **status workflow buttons** (Start Review, Approve, Deny, Mark Paid)
- Click **"File Claim"** button — show the new claim form

### 2.4 Renewal Management (45s)
- Click **Renewals** in sidebar
- Show the **Kanban board** view (Upcoming, In Progress, Quoted, Approved, Completed)
- Click on a renewal card — show the detail modal with premium change %
- Show the **"Move to"** buttons for workflow progression
- Switch to **List view** using the toggle button
- Show the table with premium comparison columns

### 2.5 Carriers (30s)
- Click **Carriers** in sidebar
- Show the carrier list with AM Best ratings and API integration status
- Click on **"Hartford Financial"** — show the detail panel with performance metrics
- Show Total Policies, Total Premium, Total Commission, Claims Ratio

### 2.6 Certificates (30s)
- Click **Certificates** in sidebar
- Click **"Generate Certificate"** — show the form (select client, policy, holder)
- Click the download icon on an existing certificate — show the COI preview modal

**Logout**

## PART 4: Analyst Persona (~1.5 min)
**Login:** `analyst@lockton.com` / `analyst123`

### 4.1 Navigation (10s)
- Show sidebar: Dashboard, Clients (read-only), Policies, Claims, Analytics, AI Assistant

### 4.2 Analytics Deep Dive (45s)
- Click **Analytics**
- Walk through all 4 tabs slowly:
  - Overview: Policy distribution, premium trends, claims, renewals
  - Loss Ratio: Industry comparison, loss ratio percentages (color-coded red/yellow/green)
  - Broker Productivity: Client counts, policy counts, premium volumes
  - Revenue Forecast: 12-month line chart projection

### 4.3 AI Assistant (30s)
- Click **AI Assistant** in sidebar
- Select a client from the dropdown (e.g., "Apex Manufacturing Co.")
- Click a suggested question like **"What coverage does Apex Manufacturing have?"**
- Wait for the AI response
- Type a custom question like **"What are the risk factors for construction companies?"**
- Show the chat interface with message history

---

## Tips for Recording
- Use **1920x1080** resolution for best quality
- Hover over elements briefly to show tooltips
- Click slowly and deliberately so viewers can follow
- Use QuickTime → File → New Screen Recording (Mac)
- Save as .MOV file
- Total runtime target: ~8-10 minutes
